Recipe to make Maggi is as follows:-
(1) Break the noodle cake into four parts.
(2) Add noodles and MaggiMasala to 1 and half glass of boiling water.
(3) Cook for 2 minutes in an open pan. Stir occasionally. Do not drain remaning water. Wait for 1 min after cooking.
(4) Your Maggi is now ready for serving or eating.
